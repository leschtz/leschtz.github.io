About me

I am Lukas Schütz, a 21 year old computer science student @TUGRAZ. My interests related to my study is data collection and microcontrollers/embedded systems.

Due to my summer job @Maxim Integrated in summer 2016, I got a little insight into the second topic and knew I want to continue working within this area.

I love to do various soft skill seminars at my university. I see them as a huge benefit, and try to improve my skills as team-player, presenter and speaker. As the english language is one of the most important lingua franca, I am doing an english course every semester. Estimated, I am somewhere in between B2 and C1. For further information, refer to my Coursework.

In my free time, I enjoy dancing - Viennese Waltz being my favourite, listening to music - especially soundtracks (Interstellar, Splinter Cell or Lord of the Rings). From time to time, I play DotA2 or CS:GO with my friends.

If you want to contact me, feel free to do this at any time: schuetz.lukas[at]sbg.at.
